Generic Rental Management

Rent out your equipment or products in Odoo through this Odoo Apps - Globalteckz Rental management module helps you to rent out your products to your customer – You can also keep track of the invoicing, expiry date, rental charges, etc. The rental management module of Odoo developed by globalteckz is strongly integrates with your accounting, warehouse, products, CRM default apps and so on.. Speed up your planning process and keep detailed track of your equipment, products, rental payments, invoices, etc.

This module will provide with below features

  • Configure rental products with price on products
  • Manage rental orders with start and end dates
  • After rental confirm generate invoice
  • After rental confirm generate Delivery Order
  • Replace products
  • Track replacement by incoming and delivery orders
  • Extend rental products
  • Configure email reminder days
